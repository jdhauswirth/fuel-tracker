🎯 Setup Auto-Launch:
Create an Apple Shortcut that automatically opens this app when you arrive within 400 meters of your fueling location.

📱 Apple Shortcuts Setup Guide

Step 1: Get Your App URL

  1. Once deployed (GitHub Pages), copy your app URL
  2. Example: https://yourusername.github.io/fuel-tracker

Step 2: Create the Shortcut

  1. Open the Shortcuts app on your iPhone
  2. Tap the + button (top right)
  3. Search for and add: "Open URL" action
  4. Paste your app URL into the URL field
  5. Name it: "Open Fuel Tracker"
  6. Tap Done

Step 3: Create Location Automation

  1. Go to the Automation tab (bottom of Shortcuts app)
  2. Tap + → Create Personal Automation
  3. Select "Arrive"
  4. Tap "Choose" next to Location
  5. Search for: 1444 S Sossaman Rd, Mesa, AZ 85209
  6. Select the location
  7. Adjust radius to 400 meters (drag the circle)
  8. Tap Next
  9. Tap Add Action
  10. Search for your shortcut: "Open Fuel Tracker"
  11. Select it
  12. Toggle OFF "Ask Before Running" (for instant launch)
  13. Tap Done

Step 4: Test It!

  1. Drive toward Costco Mesa
  2. Within 400m, your phone will automatically open the Fuel Tracker app!
  3. Make sure Location Services are enabled for Shortcuts app

✅ Pro Tips:

  • Add to Home Screen for even faster access
  • Create multiple automations for different Costco locations
  • Test the automation in the Shortcuts app before driving
  • Keep Bluetooth and WiFi on for better location accuracy

🔧 Adding More Locations:

To add automations for other Costco locations (like COSTCO GSE), repeat Step 3 with the new address. Each location gets its own automation!
